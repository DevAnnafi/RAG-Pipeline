SECTION 1: LOAN TERMS AND CONDITIONS
==============================================================================

1.1 PRINCIPAL AMOUNT
The Lender agrees to loan the Borrower the principal sum of THREE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND DOLLARS ($350,000.00).

1.2 INTEREST RATE
The loan shall bear interest at a fixed annual rate of 6.75% (six point seven five percent). This is a FIXED RATE mortgage and the interest rate will NOT change during the life of the loan.

1.3 LOAN TERM
The term of this loan is THIRTY (30) YEARS, commencing on February 1, 2024, and ending on February 1, 2054.

1.4 MONTHLY PAYMENT
Beginning March 1, 2024, the Borrower shall pay the Lender monthly installments of TWO THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SEVENTY DOLLARS AND SIXTEEN CENTS ($2,270.16), which includes principal and interest.

Payment Amount Breakdown:
- Principal and Interest: $2,270.16
- Property Tax Escrow: $425.00
- Homeowner's Insurance Escrow: $145.00
- Total Monthly Payment: $2,840.16

1.5 PAYMENT DUE DATE
Payments are due on the FIRST day of each month. A grace period of 15 days is provided. Payments received after the 16th day of the month will incur a late fee of 5% of the payment amount or $50.00, whichever is greater.

1.6 PREPAYMENT
The Borrower may prepay the loan in full or in part at any time without penalty. Prepayments will be applied first to accrued interest, then to principal.

==============================================================================
SECTION 2: PROPERTY AND COLLATERAL
==============================================================================

2.1 SECURITY INTEREST
This loan is secured by a first mortgage lien on the property located at 456 Maple Street, Springfield, MA 01101. The property includes the land, all buildings, fixtures, and improvements now or hereafter erected on the property.

2.2 PROPERTY INSURANCE
The Borrower must maintain hazard insurance on the property in an amount not less than the outstanding loan balance or the full replacement value of the improvements, whichever is greater. The insurance policy must name the Lender as mortgagee and loss payee.

Required Coverage:
- Fire and extended coverage
- Liability coverage (minimum $300,000)
- Flood insurance (if in flood zone)

2.3 PROPERTY TAXES
The Borrower must pay all property taxes and assessments when due. The Lender will maintain an escrow account for property taxes. Current annual property tax is estimated at $5,100.00.

2.4 MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Borrower must maintain the property in good condition and make all necessary repairs. The property must not be abandoned or left vacant for more than 60 consecutive days without notifying the Lender.

==============================================================================
SECTION 3: BORROWER OBLIGATIONS AND COVENANTS
==============================================================================

3.1 OCCUPANCY REQUIREMENT
The Borrower agrees to occupy the property as their principal residence within 60 days of closing and to continue to occupy the property for at least one year unless the Lender agrees otherwise in writing.

3.2 TRANSFER OF PROPERTY
The Borrower may not sell, transfer, or assign any interest in the property without the Lender's prior written consent. Any such transfer without consent may result in acceleration of the loan balance due and payable in full.

3.3 PROHIBITION AGAINST ENCUMBRANCES
The Borrower may not create additional liens or encumbrances on the property without the Lender's written consent, except for subordinate liens for improvements approved by the Lender.

3.4 HOMEOWNERS ASSOCIATION
If the property is subject to a homeowners association, the Borrower must pay all HOA fees and comply with all association rules and regulations. Current HOA fee: $85.00 per month.

==============================================================================
SECTION 4: ESCROW ACCOUNT PROVISIONS
==============================================================================

4.1 ESCROW DEPOSITS
The Lender will establish an escrow account for payment of property taxes and insurance premiums. The Borrower's monthly payment includes deposits to this account.

Initial Escrow Deposit at Closing: $1,710.00
Monthly Escrow Payment: $570.00 ($425 taxes + $145 insurance)

4.2 ESCROW ANALYSIS
The Lender will perform an annual escrow analysis. If there is a shortage, the Borrower's monthly payment may increase. If there is a surplus exceeding $50.00, the Lender will refund the excess.

==============================================================================
SECTION 5: DEFAULT AND REMEDIES
==============================================================================

5.1 EVENTS OF DEFAULT
The Borrower will be in default if any of the following occur:
(a) Failure to make any payment when due
(b) Failure to maintain required insurance
(c) Failure to pay property taxes
(d) Transfer of property without Lender consent
(e) Declaration of bankruptcy
(f) Property abandonment or significant damage

5.2 ACCELERATION CLAUSE
If the Borrower defaults, the Lender may declare the entire unpaid principal balance, accrued interest, and other charges immediately due and payable.

5.3 FORECLOSURE
Upon default and after providing required notices, the Lender may foreclose on the property through judicial or non-judicial proceedings as permitted by state law. The Borrower will be responsible for all costs of foreclosure including reasonable attorney's fees.

5.4 RIGHT TO CURE
The Borrower has thirty (30) days after receiving notice of default to cure the default before the Lender may accelerate the loan or begin foreclosure proceedings.
